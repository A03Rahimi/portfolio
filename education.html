Experience

As I was encouraged to become a computer scientist since I was a child, I started coding from a young age. I was keen to spend my time programming my Arduino board and build DIY Projects to automate my room. I was successful to use and test a variety of modules and sensors that are compatible with Arduino. In specific, some of the modules can be named as, Bluetooth module, WI-FI Module, GSM Module, Relays, and Dot Matrix. And some sensors can be mentioned as, waterflow sensor, gas sensor, temperature sensor, light sensor, ultra-sonic sensor, and more. I had applied some of them in my room and they worked. For instance I could turn the lights of my room on or off with my phone using a Bluetooth module and a relay connected to the Arduino.
